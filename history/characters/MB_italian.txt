#Patricians of Ravenna, Cesena e Forli. Decended from the Friuli/Benevento branch of the Gausi. Related to the Sassi(the Sessi/Sesso of Reggio?), Alidosi(slightly hazy, but possily through an unnamed brother of St. Romualdo or the husband of his sister) and Cavalconti(the counts of Bertinoro, not the italian ones) families(possibly also the da Polenta and Geremei but its a bit hazy how with both of them). signori di Castel Leone(near Cervia). Duca degli onestsi( Re the duca title it was more specifically their family castle between forli an cesena from where they claimed the title at first(so basically duke of the family). Like with the Traversari "duca" was maninly origanlly used by the head of the family/faction leader, around the late 10th century it became a label for the current leader of the Scola de' Pescatori/Casa Matha(the guild of Ravenna's nobles, this didn't per se makes them actual dukes of ravenna in HRE terms though, power was shared with the archbishop(who was nominally in charge) and from the late 12th century both nobles and archbishop became feudal subjects of the counts of romagna). their arms was a red lion on silver who held a golden pinenut. sometimes depicted with just the lionhead and the pinenut dangling from its mouth.
